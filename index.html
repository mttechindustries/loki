The Loki Hoodie

Minimalist hoodie with discreet IR LED integration to keep your privacy in plain sight. Perfect for tech lovers who stay low key.

Features

  • IR LEDs (850–940nm) embedded exclusively along the inside rim of the hood
  • Two additional IR LEDs positioned at the neck area
  • LEDs face inward rather than outward, creating a specific illumination pattern
  • Discreet wiring system concealed within the fabric seams
  • AAA battery holder housed in a pocket within the hoodie's main pocket
  • Soft, breathable cotton-polyester blend fabric
  • Comfortable fit with minimalist "Loki" branding

Design Advantages

  • Inside rim placement makes the technology even more subtle and hidden
  • Inward-facing LEDs create a more focused effect on the wearer's face/head
  • Internal positioning protects the LEDs from external damage
  • Installation maintains the hoodie's clean exterior aesthetic
